How to Use the System

Follow these simple steps to connect with a doctor online:

  1. Sign Up/Log In: Create an account or log in to your existing account.
  2. Describe Your Symptoms: Fill out a brief form about your health concerns.
  3. Choose a Doctor: Select a doctor from our list of available specialists.
  4. Start Consultation: Connect with the doctor via video call or chat.
  5. Get Prescription: Receive your prescription or follow-up instructions.
